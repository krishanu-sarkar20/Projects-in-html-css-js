About Attack on Titan

"Attack on Titan" is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. The story is set in a world where humanity resides within enormous walled cities to protect themselves from the Titans, gigantic humanoid creatures. The series follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they join the military to fight against the Titans and uncover the mysteries of their world.
